Introduction

I am a Front-End Designer/Developer living in the great state of North Carolina. I work from home in an office that I built, with the help of family and friends, over an 8 month period. This was my first construction project and it turned out great. It is the perfect environment to learn, create, build and ship.

Every morning when I walk into my office, I am reminded of how blessed I am to do what I love and love what I do.

I am most passionate and interested in Front-End Design and Development, but each day I gain more and more knowledge of back-end and programming technologies. When needed, I can talk the talk and walk the walk. I can also talk and walk at the same time, which is good.

Thanks to my tenacity, the web community and Google, I have taught myself design and development. If we work together and I don't know something, I will say so and then I will learn whatever is needed for that specific project.

Being self employed has given me the freedom to experiment with different tools and one of my strengths is integrating new technologies, frameworks and libraries into my development stack.

I was recently the lead designer and developer on the winning Startup Weekend Triad team, TEAL.

My availability is flexible, although I usually work from 9 am to 5 pm. I am married and have a 21 month old daughter, so it is important to balance my time between work and family.

My hourly rate is $100. This will increase accordingly as I become more proficient in various technologies and languages.

I am ready to work on great products with an even greater team. (This could be you!)
